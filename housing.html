Unit 2








Unit 2 is a traditional dorm chosen by many students at Cal-located just two blocks from campus, it's also where ROHP tends to hold its in-person programs, so its very special to us. Feel free to check out this tour, given by Denise, one of our committee members.








Blackwell Hall is a favorite option of Regents' Scholars. Renovated in 2018, it has very modern facilities, including a gym and plenty of study spaces. This tour was recorded jointly by Ana and Esther, current committee members. We hope you enjoy it!

Blackwell Hall

Martinez Commons









Martinez Commons is a predominantly apartment style living space, popular among second and third years. It also features a very modern space, with some two-story townhouse style apartments as well!
