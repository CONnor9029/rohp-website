Regents' Overnight Host Program

Welcome to the Golden Bear family

The Regents’ Overnight Host Program (ROHP) is a student-run program within the Regents’ and Chancellor's Scholars Association, an organization for current recipients of the most prestigious scholarship at the University of California, Berkeley. Each year, the ROHP Committee holds six two-part, overnight programs for candidates of the Regents’ and Chancellor's Scholarship, to provide them with a true sense of what it is like to be a Regents’ Scholar at Berkeley. Prospective Scholars will [virtually] have the chance to:

  • Experience - Experience Berkeley's campus culture and learn more about the benefits of the prestigious scholarship
  • Interact - Meet with other highly-accomplished students from all over the country, mingle with current scholars, listen to keynote faculty speakers, and view student performances
  • Discover - Embark on virtual tours, including majors-focused tours and our world-famous nighttime campus tour

For more information on ROHP, including itineraries and how to register, please explore the rest of the site. Space is limited and spots are filled on a first-come, first-served basis! Please note: you must register through Typeform to be considered fully registered for ROHP.
