Regents' Overnight Host Program

Welcome to the Golden Bear family

ROHP (Regents' Overnight Host Program) is a program put on by the Regents' and Chancellor's Scholars Association (RCSA) at the University of California, Berkeley. Every year, the ROHP committee works to put on multiple overnight host visits for prospective Regents' Scholar interviewees. Prospective scholars have the opportunity to:

  • Visit - Tour the beautiful Berkeley campus
  • Experience - Stay overnight in the dormitories with a current scholar
  • Discover - Explore one of the nation's best college towns

ROHP is an excellent program that plays a crucial role in many students' decisions to enroll at UC Berkeley. We hope that you will consider joining us at one of our Spring dates.

REGISTRATION STEPS

To register for ROHP, you must have been invited for a Regents' and Chancellor's Scholarship interview. After you apply, we will cross-check your name with a list. You must register yourself on the typeform and attach the liability waivers, community agreement, and an image of your medical insurance card. Failure to complete the waivers and upload your insurance card will result in rejection from ROHP.

FAQ'S

What should I bring?

1. A fully charged phone and a cell phone charger.
2. Photo Identification (A school ID will suffice).
3. A sleeping bag and a pillow
4. Sleeping mat if not ok with floor
5. Toiletries and a towel
6. Slippers for the showers
7. Money for personal use

Will I get off the waitlist?

If you are not within the first 10 to 15 on the waitlist, it is unlikely that you will be given a spot. Due to high demand, spaces fill up very quickly and priority will be given to those who are first on the waitlist, have majors that match the available hosts, and respond promptly to our invitation. We encourage you to look at other ROHP dates if your top choice is full.

What should my parents do?

While ROHP is a program specifically for high school students, parents often accompany their children to Berkeley. If you and your parents arrive before ROHP begins, there are campus tours or classes that you can audit. During ROHP, parents are encouraged to explore the city and campus. After the program ends, students can also visit both the UC Berkeley Botanical Gardens and BAMPFA (The Berkeley Art Museum and Pacific Film Archive). Unfortunately, we are unable to provide programming or housing for parents.

How much does ROHP cost?

ROHP is free of charge to all of our attendees, and we work hard to keep it this way! We still do recommend that students bring a small amount of cash in case they want to purchase some souvenirs or try some local eats.

Are all of the offered ROHP dates the same?

Yes, essentially. They will all follow a similar format and feature the same amount of activities. However, these will vary day by day, depending on speaker/performer availability. No program will be significantly different from another so you will not be missing out! Please check out the specific itinerary for each date if you are interested in the activities that day.

Will my transportation (plane, car, train, etc.) to Berkeley be provided?

No, we are a completely student-run program and unfortunately cannot accommodate your transportation costs. If you are OUT OF STATE, you should contact Beth Berends at the scholarship office.

I no longer want to/am able to attend ROHP but I have already registered on Typeform. What should I do?

Please email us at contact@rohp.berkeley.edu ASAP so we can let others who want to attend off the waitlist.

ROHP is full! Is there any way I can still visit Berkeley?

Of course! You are welcome to come to Berkeley during the day, speak to the scholarship office, and take a guided campus tour. Additionally, Cal has a number of other overnight host programs, including the Overnight Stay Program (OSP), which may be able to accommodate you.
