REGISTRATION STEPS

To register for ROHP, you must be a candidate for the Regents’ and Chancellor’s Scholarship. We will cross-check your name with a list from the scholarship office. Please register for a program through one of the Typeforms linked below. About 5 days before your registered program date, a Zoom link will be sent to the email address you submitted on the Typeform that is registered with your Zoom account.

  • Please find the required event waivers linked below. You will need to fill out and sign these forms, and then scan and upload the signed copies when you sign up through the Typeforms linked below.

REGISTRATION FORM

  • Below are the dates to each ROHP night. Each date links to a Typeform that will allow you to register for the night of your choosing. Please only register for one night; if you have registered for more than one program, we will have to cancel all registrations under your name. If you were nominated for the Regents’ and Chancellor’s Scholarship and received an invitation to interview for the Scholarship, the first five programs are open for registration.

    The April 16th-17th Program is for Scholarship Recipients only, but will contain the same programming as prior dates. If you have already attended one of the first five programs, please do not sign up for the 16th-17th. If a form is closed, that means we have filled up all of the guest spots for that program and you may register for the waitlist here. Make sure to fill out the waitlist form multiple times if you would like to be on the waitlist for multiple nights.
  • To access the registration Typeforms, please click on one of the bears with the program date you desire to attend.
