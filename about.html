ROHP (Regents’ Overnight Host Program) is a program put on by the Regents’ and Chancellor’s Scholars Association (RCSA) at the University of California, Berkeley. Every year, the ROHP Committee puts on six overnight visits for Regents’ and Chancellor’s Scholarship candidates to provide a fun and authentic Berkeley experience. Obviously, things are a little different due to COVID-19, but normally, prospective scholars have the opportunity to:

  • Visit - Tour the beautiful Berkeley campus
  • Experience - Stay overnight in the dormitories with a current scholar
  • Discover - Explore one of the nation's best college towns
  • Interact - Meet professors, scholars, and student organizations

For more information on ROHP, including itineraries and how to register, please scroll through the rest of the site. Space is limited and spots are filled on a first-come, first-served basis! Please note: you must register through Typeform to be considered fully registered for ROHP.
