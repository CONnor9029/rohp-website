Shukri Suhir
Shukri Suhir is a fourth-year undergraduate student studying Public Health and Data Science at UC Berkeley. As an Undergraduate Researcher with Dr. Coco Auersweld's Youth and Allies Against Homelessness Lab, he has studied the effect of COVID-19 on unhoused youth and more recently the impact of HHAP set-aside funding for youth homelessness in California. He is also an Outreach Coordinator for Suitcase Clinic, a student-run organization that offers free health and social services to underserved and unhoused populations in the city of Berkeley. Outside of campus, Shukri also works part-time as an Emergency Medical Technician in San Francisco. He is very passionate about increasing awareness of homelessness as a major Public Health issue and approaches research, healthcare, and advocacy through a lens of equity and social justice.
