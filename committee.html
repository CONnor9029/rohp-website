Sarah Truong
Hey there!!! My name is Sarah Truong, and I am one of the coordinators leading ROHP this year! I am a fourth-year from Anaheim, CA studying Earth and Planetary Science, Data Science, and Computer Science! Other campus organizations that I'm a part of include the Computer Science Scholars Program, Association of Women in EE&CS, Atmospheric Science Association at Berkeley, and the Data Science Scholars Program. I was the youngest undergraduate instructor for the award-wining course of over 900 students, Introduction to General Astronomy (Astro C10)! I am an undergraduate student researcher for two different research labs, one of which is led by Nobel laureate in Physics, Saul Perlmutter, at the Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory! In my free time, I love to go on spontaneous trips with my friends, clown around, listen to the "Binge Mode" podcast, watch the sunset at the beach, go stargazing, curate Spotify playlists, watch NBA basketball games, and interior design my friends' apartments! I love meeting prospective Scholars, so feel free to reach out to me with any questions!
