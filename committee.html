Ya Chi "Angelina" Liang
Hi there! I'm Angelina (she/her) and I'm a fourth-year from Los Angeles, CA studying Psychology and Molecular Cell Biology with an emphasis in Immunology and Pathogenesis. On campus, I work with industry and community partners to develop solutions for public health challenges through the Fung Fellowship, guide and support high school students in navigating the college application process as an Advising Fellow with Matriculate, and serve as the Economic Empowerment Program Assistant at the YWCA Berkeley/Oakland chapter, a non-profit organization dedicated to eliminating racism and providing free informational programming and resources for community building. I'm a Research Assistant in the Substance Abuse Research Program at UCSF and in the Idiographic Dynamics Lab here at Cal where I study harm reduction and service expansion for under-resourced communities. In my free time, I enjoy painting, creating Spotify playlists, and refining my skincare routine. Feel free to reach out with any questions at all!
