Dominic Gutierrez
Ello!!! My name is Dominic Gutierrez (he/him) and I'm a fourth-year, first-gen student studying History and Chicana/o Studies. Ya Chi "Angelina" Liang
Hi there! I'm Angelina (she/her) and I'm a fourth-year from Los Angeles, CA studying Psychology and Molecular Cell Biology with an emphasis in Immunology and Pathogenesis. On campus, I work with industry and community partners to develop solutions for public health challenges through the Fung Fellowship, guide and support high school students in navigating the college application process as an Advising Fellow with Matriculate, and serve as the Economic Empowerment Program Assistant at the YWCA Berkeley/Oakland chapter, a non-profit organization dedicated to eliminating racism and providing free informational programming and resources for community building. I'm a Research Assistant in the Substance Abuse Research Program at UCSF and in the Idiographic Dynamics Lab here at Cal where I study harm reduction and service expansion for under-resourced communities. In my free time, I enjoy painting, creating Spotify playlists, and refining my skincare routine. Feel free to reach out with any questions at all!

Esther Jung
Hi besties! I'm Esther and I'm a second year double majoring in Public Health & Molecular Cell Biology (Neurobiology emphasis) on the pre-med track. I grew up in San Diego, California, but I have also lived in Pennsylvania, Idaho, and Minnesota! I'm involved in URAP as a patient support intern at the UCSF Medical Center, and I'm also a research assistant in the Colford-Hubbard Group at the School of Public Health, where I do data science work on publications investigating global public health crises. I'm a TA for Public Health 116, which is a seminar on social, political, and ethical issues in public health and medicine. I coordinate the Tang Center Outreach Team and CARE, a volunteer club that combines music and therapy. On the weekends, you'll find me playing violin in the UC Berkeley Symphony Orchestra concerts, at the gym, grocery shopping at Trader Joe's, cooking, and making pit stops at Philz Coffee! I have lots to share about choosing Cal, learning my boundaries and balancing my college lifestyle, navigating intro courses, and passions outside of my majors like music. You are welcome to email me/reach out at anytime!
